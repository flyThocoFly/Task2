"Wubba lubba dub-dub" actually means, "help me, I'm in great pain," in Birdperson's language, as Rick's bird companion informs Morty during the episode "Ricksy Business." Though Rick first utters the catchphrase in season 1's "Meeseeks and Destroy," "Wubba lubba dub-dub" remains a recurring joke in the series. At the same time, it's a cry for help that betrayed Rick's inner thoughts — before he actually made breakthroughs in the show. As well as being memorable, it's also tied to one of the earliest moments in Rick & Morty that shows Rick Sanchez is a much more troubled character than he first appears.
